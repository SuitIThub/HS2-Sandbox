# Anim Browser — grouping animations

Bundle related animations into **one card** with role buttons (m/f, in/loop/out).

![Select animations with checkboxes and open Group selected](images/anim-browser/ab-11-group-selected.gif)

> **Lesbian → Rear Desk Cunnilingus**: check two **About To Begin** cards (**2 selected**) → **Group selected…** → **Group Review** assigns **f** slots **1** and **2**, **Save as: About To Begin** → **Confirm**.

## Steps

1. Check **checkbox** on two or more animation cards
2. **Group selected…** in content action bar
3. [Review panel](Review-Panel) opens — confirm gender/phase roles
4. **Confirm** — animations share one tile

Content bar also offers **Capture thumbnail(s)…** for checked cards and **Rename…** for a single selection.

## Ungroup

Select grouped card → **Ungroup** — splits back to separate animations. Original Studio data unchanged.

## Role inference

Gender (m/f) and phase (in/loop/out) guessed from names — always editable in review.
