## Single click apply

Clicking an animation applies it to **every** character currently selected in Studio.

## Grouped cards

Some tiles bundle related animations. Role buttons on the card:

| Button | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **m / f** (m2, f2…) | Male/female or participant slot |
| **in / loop / out** | Intro, loop, outro phase |
| **1 / 2 / 3…** | Numbered variants |

Grouped cards come from your [Grouping](Grouping) or [merge review](Review-Panel).

## Select without applying

Use card **checkbox** to select for grouping or **Capture thumbnail(s)…** without applying. Content action bar shows count and actions.

## Multi-character & paired animations

When several characters are selected, [Characters pane](Characters-and-Options) priority decides who gets which role (m/f) on paired animations.

Wrong role? Use **m / f** buttons on the card or reorder Characters list.
